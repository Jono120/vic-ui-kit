Major requirements

In addition to the degree requirements [link to degree page], to graduate with a BSc with a major in Physics you will need to include:

Four courses at 100 level:
  • Either (Calculus 1B (MATH 142) and Algebra (MATH 151)) or (Engineering Mathematics Foundations (ENGR 121) and B+ or better in Engineering Mathematics with Calculus (ENGR 122))
  • Physics 1A (PHYS 114)
  • Physics 1B (PHYS 115)
Five courses at 200 level:
  • Multivariable Calculus (MATH 243)

Three courses from:

  • Quantum Mechanics and Kinetic Theory (PHYS 241)
  • Electromagnetism I (PHYS 242)
  • Classical Mechanics and Relativity (PHYS 243)
  • Methods of Experimental Physics (PHYS 245)

One further course from:

  • EEEN 201-204
  • PHYS 201-259
Four courses at 300 level:
  • Electromagnetism II (PHYS 304)
  • Thermal Physics (PHYS 305)
  • Quantum Physics (PHYS 307)
  • Solid State and Nuclear Physics (PHYS 309)
