Research centres, institutes and chairs

  • Architecture

    With an impressive breadth of collaborations, we champion creativity and exploration. From urban design to wearable technologies, this faculty embodies innovation.

    • Centre of Building Performance Research (CBPR)
    • Earthquake Hazard Centre (EHC)

  • Business

    At the heart of New Zealand’s business, finance, public and government community, our research expertise supports local and international solutions.

    • BNZ Chair in Business in Asia
    • Brian Picot Chair in Ethical Leadership
    • Centre for Accounting Governance and Taxation Research
    • Centre for Labour, Employment and Work (CLEW)
    • Chair in the Economics of Disasters
    • Chair in Public Finance
    • Competitive Advantage New Zealand

  • Engineering

    Our engineering, computer and mathematical science researchers are at the forefront of their fields. Work on pure and applied solutions have global relevance.
    • Computational Media Innovation Centre (opening June 2018)
    • Robinson Research Institute

  • Environment

    We are a Southern hemisphere leader in several areas of conservation biology, geophysics, climate change and geochemistry.
    • Centre for Biodiversity and Restoration Ecology
    • Institute of Geophysics
    • New Zealand Climate Change Institute
    • Victoria University Coastal Ecology Lab

  • Government

    At the legislative centre of New Zealand, our research staff work at the forefront of judicial, governance and democratic consultancy and response.
    • Centre For Strategic Studies
    • Chair in Digital Government
    • Diana Unwin Chair in Restorative Justice
    • Chair of Wellbeing and Public Policy
    • Institute of Governance and Policy studies
    • New Zealand Centre for Public Law
    • Roy Mckenzie Centre for the Study of Families
    • Treaty of Waitangi Research Unit

  • Health

    Our researchers collaborate with businesses and scientists around the world to develop solutions for the prevention, treatment and cure of disease.
    • Centre for Biodiscovery
    • Ferrier
    • Health Services Research Centre
    • Malaghan Institute of Medical Research

  • Humanities

    Our internationally acclaimed researchers innovate across many disciplines from political science through media and psychology to linguistics.
    • Centre for Strategic Studies
    • Confucius Institute
    • Deaf Studies Research Unit
    • International Institute of Modern Letters
    • New Zealand Centre for Literary Translation
    • New Zealand Contemporary China Research Centre
    • New Zealand Dictionary Centre
    • New Zealand India Research Institute
    • New Zealand Institute of International Affairs
    • Stout Research Centre
    • Treaty of Waitangi Research Unit
    • Victoria Institute For Links With Latin America (Villa)
    • Wai-te-ata Press

  • Law

    Victoria is New Zealand’s leading research centre for policy, governance and law. Connections with government, communities, NGOs and commerce facilitates global solutions.
    • New Zealand Association for Comparative Law
    • New Zealand Centre for Public Law
    • New Zealand Centre of International Economic Law

  • Science

    With unique geological research, geochemistry, terrestrial, galactic and molecular expertise, the science faculty partners with international bodies on cutting edge projects.

    • Centre for Biodiversity and Restoration Ecology
    • Centre for Applied Cross-cultural Research
    • Centre for Logic, Language and Computation
    • Ferrier
    • Institute of Geophysics
    • MacDiarmid Institute for Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology
    • New Zealand Climate Change Institute
    • Robinson Research Institute
    • Roy Mckenzie Centre for The Study of Families
    • Victoria University Coastal Ecology Lab
