Key dates and University Calendar

Take a look at the University’s key dates and Calendar.

University Calendar

The University Calendar is a yearly publication which includes dates, deadlines, contact information, statutes, policies, course of study statutes and general information.

Trimester dates

Victoria University of Wellington’s academic year is divided into three trimesters:

  • Trimester 1 (1/3): March–July
  • Trimester 2 (2/3): July–November
  • Trimester 3 (3/3): November–February
