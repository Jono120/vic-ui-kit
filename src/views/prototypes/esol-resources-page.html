Practise your English online

LLC staff have collected and regularly check the weblinks below with one goal in mind— to help you improve your English online.

Remember, the LLC has lots of offline practice materials for all language skills available in the Self-access Library and on LLC computers. Ask staff at the LLC counter. If you have any feedback on this website, we would like to hear from you! Happy practising!
