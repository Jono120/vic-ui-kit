Nau mai ki te Paepae Rauemi.

He wāhi rapu rauemi whakaako kua hangaia mai e Te Kura Māori ki Akopai.

This resource platform houses a range of teaching resources and iwi stories that have been created to support kura and schools across Aotearoa. You will find e-books developed for the Tuia Mātauranga Project funded by the Ministry of Education to support the commemoration of the arrival of Captain Cook to Aotearoa in 1869.

We have been privileged to work with iwi across the country to develop and share their stories with Aotearoa. Ka nui te mihi!

You will also find an interactive flipchart download that can be accessed with the ActivInspire software. This resource, He Manu Tuhituhi—he ara pāhekoheko, was originally developed by Aronui Ltd for the Ministry of Education to support the teaching of Tuhituhi in Māori medium Kura.

Be sure to visit our Taupanga | App suite to download your free desktop app version of KURA, KURA ENGLISH and PUNA.

These game-based language Apps are a great way to learn the language!
